# Reference — The Double (concept)

A high-level abstract of pepl's central concept and what we cooked up in research.

## What a Double is

A **semantic model of a person** — it learns to sound like them and represents them. **Voice fidelity is the product**: if it doesn't sound like you, nothing else matters. It's a mirror you test — you're both the subject and the judge.

## The flow (concept)

1. **Surface / reveal** — from minimal signal it forms a v0: *"I think you're someone who…"* — specific, not a horoscope. The hook.
2. **Test** — you interact; it responds *as you*; you catch the misses; each good beat builds trust.
3. **Reclaim** — you correct and own how it represents you. (The conscious mind reclaiming meaning from what the unconscious surfaced.)
4. **Connect** — others see how your Double sees *them*; stories juxtapose (self-view vs how-others-see-you) and link into a shared world.

## Storytelling is the medium

It triangulates the story you *tell*, the story you *live*, and the story you *narrate*, and surfaces the gaps as grounded receipts. It sells **receipts, not transformation** — every claim traces to something real.

## The connection / world (the differentiator)

The magic is multiplayer: send someone your snapshot and they see how your Double sees *them* — the self-view vs friend-view juxtaposition. Individual reclaimed stories link into a connected world.

## The live world

A real-time, **spatial** visualization of the Double forming — watching it happen, not a progress bar — so the process feels alive.

## Grounding (non-negotiable)

A Double is only credible if **every claim traces back to a real interaction**. Ungrounded = cut. This is the truth gate — the same discipline as our research on grounded stories.
